This is an interactive and responsive website used to order pizzas, side dishes and beverages. The website has not only pre-existing menus but also lets the customer create their own pizzas by choosing their preferred crust and toppings. Once the user adds what they want to order, they can cross check that on the 'Cart' webpage. When they are ready, they can place the order and pay using their Card or through their E-Wallet.There is also a webpage for admins to make changes like adding or deleting items from the website.
